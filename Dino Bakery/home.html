Raspberry Panna Cotta

Panna Cotta is a simple Italian dessert made by adding gelatin to cooked cream. Our version is sweetened with lots of fresh raspberries, a bit of sugar, and a hint of lemon juice. Want to try purple panna cotta instead of pink? Just swap the raspberries for blackberries.
